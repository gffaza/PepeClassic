How To Get Pepe Classic

  1. Create a Wallet Download metamask or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to metamask.io.
  2. Get Some ETH Having ETH in your wallet to switch to Pepe Classic. If you don’t have any ETH, you can buy directly on metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.
  3. Add contract address in your wallet Add Pepe Classic contract address to your account.
